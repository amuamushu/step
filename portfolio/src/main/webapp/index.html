Background

I am an instructional assistant for the data structures and algorithms course at my university. I absolutely love it because I get to help people learn about my favorite subject and I find it extremely rewarding to help students understand the course material.

I attended Google’s Computer Science Summer Institute (CSSI) back in Summer 2018. It was my favorite summer experience to date because I was able to meet so many talented, kind, and driven individuals from all across the US. Their ambition motivated me to push myself harder in college than in high school and I’m extremely grateful to have met them. We still keep in touch too! :D

I’m from Garden Grove, California. It’s a city in southern California, known for its large Vietnamese population. Hence, it is also the location of the largest Little Saigon in America. Growing up here, I was surrounded by Vietnamese culture and am very fortunate to experience its traditions in a community that is very accepting of it.
